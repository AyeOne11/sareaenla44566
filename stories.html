Del-Ruel

Bored with their posh lives, three noble Alorerian women—Tessa, Elisa, and Marie—embark on an illicit adventure to Del-Ruel, a restricted nation shunned for its open sexual culture and thriving black-market trade.   Seeking thrills before their academic futures at the Royal Academy begin, they immerse themselves in a world of debauchery, advanced technology, and unexpected self-discovery[cite: 523, 575]. Will they embrace this forbidden paradise or succumb to its dangers?

COMING SOON - The Spiral

Lyra's descent into addiction begins innocently after she is given a free sample of a new "miracle cure" for everyday woes. Unbeknownst to her, this product is finely ground White Crystal, a highly addictive and illicit substance smuggled from Del-Ruel  .  As her reliance grows, she spirals through black-market dealings, family neglect, and spiritual corruption, ultimately facing defilement and homelessness.

This cautionary tale delves into the destructive grip of substance abuse and the quest for redemption in a world of temptation.
